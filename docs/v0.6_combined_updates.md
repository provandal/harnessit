## What did *not* change

These items were considered and deliberately left as in earlier versions:

- The seven-component harness model (§3 in Architecture). v0.6 elaborates §3.1 and §3.8 but does not add or remove components.
- The Substrate Adapter / Driver / Adapter shell three-layer model. v0.6 names a session-cache property of the contract but does not change the shape.
- The 60-day / 4-month / 12-month tier targets. Tier-0 is now HIT; Tier-1 and Tier-2 are unchanged.
- The Doppelgänger license boundary (GPL-2.0 substrate, Apache-2.0 Adapter, runtime composition). v0.3 §9.5 is identical to v0.2.
- The Doppelgänger inet-tub fork choice. v0.3 §9.2 advances the pinned SHA but does not revisit the choice.
- The Langfuse staged-adoption commitment (Cloud for stages 0–3, self-hosted from stage 4). Stage 4b empirically validated the substrate-substitution pattern; the commitment stands.
- The RememberIT dogfooding framing (Principle 7 disclosure). v0.6 preserves the framing exactly.
